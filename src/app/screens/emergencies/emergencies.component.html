Emergency protocols
Quick reference for emergency cardiac presentations and claim procedures
⚠
Emergency claims at non-DSP facilities require retrospective pre-authorisation within 48 hours. Notify the scheme immediately on 0800 222 333.
Quick reference
Acute STEMI (I21.0-I21.3)
Action: Activate cath lab, PCI within 90 min, aspirin and clopidogrel loading, heparin.
Claim: PMB condition funded from the risk pool. No co-pay at DSP.
Acute pulmonary oedema (I50.1)
Action: IV furosemide, oxygen, GTN if stable, CPAP if available, admit ICU.
Claim: PMB condition with in-hospital authorisation.
Complete heart block (I44.2)
Action: Atropine, pacing, urgent temporary wire, emergency pacemaker if indicated.
Claim: PMB emergency, fast-tracked panel review.
Ventricular fibrillation / cardiac arrest (I49.0)
Action: Immediate CPR, defibrillation, ACLS protocol, adrenaline and amiodarone.
Claim: All resuscitation costs covered with retrospective auth.
Aortic dissection (I71.0)
Action: IV beta-blocker, CT angiogram, urgent surgery for type A.
Claim: PMB emergency with ICU and surgical codes covered.
