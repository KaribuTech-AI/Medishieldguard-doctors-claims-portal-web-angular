Scheme rules reference
Key Medical Schemes Act provisions and MediShieldGuard benefit rules
Rule 5.2 - Co-payments and deductibles
Members using DSP providers pay no co-payment within benefit limits. Non-DSP usage carries a 20% co-payment except for PMB conditions handled correctly.
Rule 7.1 - Pre-authorisation requirements
Admissions, higher-cost procedures, and specified investigations require pre-authorisation. Emergency admissions must be notified within 48 hours.
Rule 8.3 - DSP network and billing
DSP providers must bill at or below NHRPL tariff rates. Balance billing to DSP members is prohibited. Claims must be submitted electronically within 30 days.
Rule 10.1 - Chronic Disease List
CDL conditions are funded from the risk pool. Patients must be registered on the DMP and non-formulary medication requires motivation.
Rule 12.2 - Provider payments and timelines
Clean DSP claims are settled within 3 business days via EFT. Disputed claims are handled within 60 days of dispute lodgement.
